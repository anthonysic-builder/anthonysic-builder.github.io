Historical Overview

The World War II service of Pvt. Paul Costa reflects the massive logistical effort required to sustain the Allied victory. His journey began as a heavy truck driver with the 8th Air Force in England, hauling munitions and supplies for the strategic bombing campaign.

As the war in Europe culminated, he transferred to the 257th Engineer Combat Battalion (7th Army), serving in Germany during the final days of the war and the subsequent Occupation, helping to rebuild a shattered continent.
